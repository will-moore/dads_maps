About "Dad's maps"

This is a simple tool for keeping track of the Ordnance Survey Landranger maps that you own, and showing the coverage of them across the UK.
The checked maps are automatically saved in the browser on your device, even when you close the window, so you don't need to save as you go.

The idea for this came from a recent visit home, when my Dad was trying to sort his maps, together with me hearing about the availability of Ordnance Survey Open Data.

I found that the Landranger sheet names and coordinates are not publicly available but after asking for the data on their forums, the Ordnance Survey team eventually prepared and sent me a spreadsheet of everything I needed! So thank you very much to them!

Then it was just a case of using the OS Open Space API to build this app, which you can find on github.

Enjoy it!

Any feedback etc please contact me at @will_j_moore
